                 INTRODUCTION
ULTRACTION General Contracting is a leader in civil construction and concrete struc-
ture works, built on a foundation of excellence, innovation, and reliability.

Committed to delivering high performance results, we integrate value
engneering to optimize efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and quality in every project.


Our expert team comprising skilled engineers, tradesmen, and dedicated
professionals adopts a hands-on approach to ensure seamless execution while uphold-
ing the highest safety.
standards.


Sustainability is at the core of our operations, as we implement eco-conscious
practices that minimize environmental impact and promote long-term
resilience. Beyond construction, we embrace social responsibility by fostering ethical
business practices, supporting local communities, and prioritizing workplace
safety and well-being. With a culture of continuous improvement and a commitment
to trust and collaboration, we build not just structures, but lasting relationships that
shape a sustainable and responsible future.

                                                                         SERVICES



 BRIDGES, UNDERPASSES AND BOX CULVERTS
 We specialize in bridges, underpasses, and box culverts, offering end-to-end
 services from planning to construction to handover. With a focus on safety,
 aesthetics, and functionality, we deliver excellence in structural engineering,
 enhancing resilient transportation and water management systems.



 INDUSTRIAL, COMMERCIAL AND RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS
 We specialize in constructing industrial, commercial, residential buildings, and palaces, offering
 tailored services from planning to execution. Combining expertise and innovation, we deliver
 projects that meet industry standards, fulfill functional needs, and reflect aesthetic aspirations.
 From resilient and efficient built environments to opulent palaces that epitomize grandeur and
 timeless elegance, our commitment to excellence ensures every structure is crafted with
 precision and artistry.




 W ATE R RESERVOIR AND PUMP STATIONS
 We specialize in water reservoirs and pump stations, delivering end-to-end services from
 design to maintenance for efficient water management. Combining construction expertise with
 a focus on sustainability, we provide reliable, tailored solutions to meet the varied
 requirements of water supply projects.




 POWER PLANTS & DISTRIBUTION SUBSTATION AND CONTROL
 BUILDINGS (CIVIL WORKS)
 We specialize in civil works for distribution substations and control buildings, offering complete services
 from planning to maintenance. With a focus on precision and strict adherence to safety, functionality,
 and reliability standards, we ensure the seamless and efficient operation of vital utility infrastructure.




CIVIL WORKS FOR SEAPORTS AND AIRPORTS
We specialize in civil works for seaports and airports, offering end-to-end services from
planning to construction to support critical infrastructure development. With a focus on
precision and compliance with international standards, we enhance the seamless and
resilient operations of key transportation hubs in both maritime and aviation sectors.
                                                      SERVICES


ONSHORE AND OFFSHORE CIVIL WORKS (OIL AND GAS
FIELD)
We specialize in onshore and offshore civil works for the oil and gas sector,
providing end-to-end services in planning, design, and execution of essential
infrastructure on land and at sea. With a strong focus on safety, durability,
and environmental compliance,     we     meet      stringent  industry standards,
supporting the success and sustainability of oil and gas operations.



  HERITAGE REVITALIZATION PROJECT
  Committed to heritage revitalization, we focus on preserving and restoring
  historical and cultural landmarks. Using advanced restoration techniques while
  honoring historical authenticity, we thoughtfully integrate modern amenities
  where needed, ensuring these cherished landmarks remain relevant and
  sustainable for future generations.




 PIPELINE AND STORM NE TW OR K
 Specializing in Pipeline and Storm Network projects, we provide end-to-end
 services ensuring efficient transportation of substances and storm water
 management. With expertise in industry standards and environmental
 considerations, we innovate in every project to contribute to sustainable urban
 and industrial environments, establishing vital pipelines and robust storm water
 networks.




 R OAD WORKS AND EARTHWORKS

 We specialize in road works and earthworks, providing expert services from
 planning to execution with a commitment to quality and strict compliance with
 regulations.




UTILITIES
We specialize in utility infrastructure development, offering comprehensive services
from planning to execution to handover. With a commitment to safety, efficiency, and
sustainability, we deliver robust solutions for utility systems that enhance community
resilience and support essential services. From water distribution and drainage
systems to power and communication lines, our expertise ensures functionality,
reliability, and seamless integration into broader infrastructure networks.
