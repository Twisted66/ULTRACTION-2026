# Water Reservoir & 6 Power Stations Samha

Combined water storage and power distribution infrastructure serving the Samha district of Abu Dhabi.

## Project Overview

This project involved constructing a water reservoir and six power distribution stations to serve the growing Samha district with reliable water and electricity infrastructure.

## Key Features

- Underground water reservoir
- 6 electrical power distribution stations
- Interconnecting utility corridors
- SCADA monitoring systems
- Site access and security systems

## Technical Specifications

- Reservoir capacity: 10 million gallons
- Power stations: 11kV/415V distribution
- Monitoring: Central SCADA system
- Construction: Reinforced concrete
- Security: Fenced compound with access control
