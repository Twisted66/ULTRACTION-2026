# Offshore Oil & Gas Field Development

Offshore infrastructure project supporting oil and gas field operations with platforms, pipelines, and support facilities.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of offshore infrastructure to support oil and gas field operations, including platform foundations, pipeline corridors, and support facilities.

## Key Features

- Offshore platform foundations
- Subsea pipeline installations
- Onshore support facilities
- Marine vessel coordination
- Safety and environmental systems

## Technical Specifications

- Foundation type: Steel piles with concrete caps
- Pipeline diameter: 24" to 48" steel pipes
- Support facilities: 5,000 square meters
- Safety systems: Fire suppression and emergency response
- Environmental: Oil spill prevention and response
