# Upgrading of Inner Bypass Parallel to E-45

Comprehensive road upgrade project along the Inner Bypass parallel to E-45, significantly improving capacity and safety.

## Project Overview

This major infrastructure project involved upgrading the entire Inner Bypass road running parallel to E-45, including widening, intersection improvements, drainage systems, and complete street infrastructure.

## Key Features

- Road widening from 2 to 4 lanes
- New intersection configurations
- Complete drainage systems
- Street lighting and signage
- Safety barriers and pedestrian facilities

## Technical Specifications

- Length: 15 kilometers
- Lanes: 2 to 4 lanes each direction
- Intersections: 8 major intersections upgraded
- Drainage: 15km of new storm drains
- Lighting: LED street lighting throughout
