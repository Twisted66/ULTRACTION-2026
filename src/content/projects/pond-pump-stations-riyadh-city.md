# Pond & Pump Stations Riyadh City

Storm water management system with detention ponds and pump stations serving the Riyadh City district.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of storm water detention ponds and associated pump stations to manage surface water runoff and prevent flooding in the Riyadh City area.

## Key Features

- Storm water detention ponds
- Electric pump stations
- Underground drainage network
- Water quality treatment systems
- Monitoring and control systems

## Technical Specifications

- Pond capacity: 20,000 cubic meters
- Pump capacity: 2 x 500 m3/hour pumps
- Drainage network: 5km of pipes
- Control system: SCADA integration
- Safety: Fencing and warning systems
