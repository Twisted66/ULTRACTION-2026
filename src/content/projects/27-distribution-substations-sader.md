# 27 Distribution Substations Sader

Comprehensive electrical infrastructure project involving the construction of 27 distribution substations in the Sader district of Abu Dhabi.

## Project Overview

This project involved the design and construction of 27 electrical distribution substations to support the growing power demands of the Sader district. The substations were strategically located to optimize power distribution across the area.

## Key Features

- 27 distribution substations built to ADC specifications
- High-voltage equipment installation and testing
- Advanced SCADA system integration
- Comprehensive safety and protection systems

## Technical Specifications

- Capacity: Each substation designed for 11kV distribution
- Safety systems: Fire suppression, ventilation, and emergency power
- Control systems: Remote monitoring and control capabilities
