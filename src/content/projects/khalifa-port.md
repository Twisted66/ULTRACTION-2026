# Khalifa Port Phase 2

Expansion of Khalifa Port, one of the region's premier deep-water ports, with additional quay walls, storage areas, and support facilities.

## Project Overview

This phase 2 expansion project significantly increased the port's capacity with new quay construction, container storage areas, and supporting infrastructure.

## Key Features

- Additional 1,200 meters of quay wall
- Deepened berths for larger vessels
- Container storage and handling areas
- Support buildings and administration facilities
- Road and utility infrastructure

## Technical Specifications

- Quay length: 1,200 meters additional
- Water depth: 17 meters draft
- Storage area: 350,000 square meters
- Pavement: Reinforced concrete for heavy container handling
