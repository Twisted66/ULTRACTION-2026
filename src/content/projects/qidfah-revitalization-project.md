# Qidfah Revitalization Project

Heritage revitalization project preserving and restoring the historical and cultural landmarks of Qidfah in Fujairah.

## Project Overview

This project involves the preservation and restoration of historical and cultural landmarks in Qidfah, Fujairah. Using advanced restoration techniques, we integrated modern amenities while honoring the authenticity of the original structures.

## Key Features

- Historical landmark preservation and restoration
- Integration of modern amenities with traditional architecture
- Use of advanced restoration techniques
- Cultural heritage protection
- Sustainable tourism infrastructure

## Technical Specifications

- Heritage structures restored: Multiple buildings
- Restoration technique: Traditional methods with modern reinforcement
- Materials: Locally sourced stone and traditional materials
- Completion: 2024
