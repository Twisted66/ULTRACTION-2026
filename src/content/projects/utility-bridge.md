# Construction of Utility Bridge

Specialized utility bridge constructed to carry essential services across major roadways in Abu Dhabi.

## Project Overview

This utility bridge was designed to carry water, electrical, and telecommunications services across a major highway, minimizing ground-level disruptions and providing safe passage for maintenance personnel.

## Key Features

- Dedicated utility corridor for multiple services
- Maintenance walkway with safety railings
- Weather protection for utility lines
- Access points for maintenance vehicles
- Fire protection systems for electrical cables

## Technical Specifications

- Span: 65 meters across the highway
- Width: 8 meters utility corridor
- Height clearance: 7.5 meters above road
- Load capacity: Designed for utility vehicles
