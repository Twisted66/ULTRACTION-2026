# Huge Water Reservoir & 29 Substations MBZ City

Major water and electrical infrastructure project serving Mohammed Bin Zayed City with a massive water reservoir and 29 distribution substations.

## Project Overview

This comprehensive infrastructure project involved constructing a large water reservoir and 29 electrical distribution substations to serve the growing needs of MBZ City in Abu Dhabi.

## Key Features

- Underground water reservoir with 50 million gallon capacity
- 29 electrical distribution substations
- Integrated pumping station
- Advanced water treatment systems
- SCADA monitoring and control

## Technical Specifications

- Reservoir capacity: 50 million gallons
- Reservoir dimensions: 100m x 80m x 8m deep
- Substations: 11kV/415V distribution
- Pumping capacity: 5,000 m3/hour
- Construction: Reinforced concrete with waterproofing
