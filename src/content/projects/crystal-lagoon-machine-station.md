# Crystal Lagoon & Machine Station Maydan

Premier leisure destination featuring a stunning crystal lagoon with state-of-the-art water treatment and circulation systems.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of a large-scale crystal lagoon at Maydan, complete with a sophisticated machine station housing water filtration, circulation, and treatment systems.

## Key Features

- Crystal clear lagoon with advanced filtration
- Machine station with pumps and filtration systems
- Water treatment and chemical dosing systems
- Beach areas and recreational spaces
- Landscaped surroundings with pedestrian pathways

## Technical Specifications

- Lagoon area: 50,000 square meters
- Water capacity: 75,000 cubic meters
- Filtration system: Sand filters with UV treatment
- Circulation pumps: 6 x 150hp units
- Water quality: Continuous monitoring system
