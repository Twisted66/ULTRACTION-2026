# Baniyas North Infrastructure

Complete infrastructure development for the Baniyas North district, including roads, utilities, and public facilities.

## Project Overview

This comprehensive infrastructure project involved the development of roads, drainage systems, electrical networks, and landscaping for the Baniyas North residential district.

## Key Features

- Road network construction with asphalt paving
- Storm water drainage systems
- Electrical and telecommunications infrastructure
- Street lighting and traffic signaling
- Landscaping and hardscaping works

## Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: Coordinating multiple utility services

### Solution: Implemented detailed BIM coordination and scheduled utility installations in phases to minimize conflicts.
