# Distribution Substation Aqah

Modern electrical distribution substation serving the Aqah region of Fujairah with reliable power infrastructure.

## Project Overview

This distribution substation was built to enhance the electrical infrastructure in Fujairah's Aqah region, providing reliable power distribution to residential and commercial developments.

## Key Features

- 11kV/415V distribution transformers
- High-voltage switchgear installation
- SCADA integration for remote monitoring
- Fire protection and safety systems
- Backup power supply capability

## Technical Specifications

- Transformer capacity: 2 x 25 MVA
- Voltage levels: 11kV primary, 415V secondary
- Switchgear: SF6 insulated type
- Protection: Numerical relay protection system
