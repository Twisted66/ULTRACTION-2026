# Etihad Rail 2D Package Huge Water Tanks

Massive water storage tank construction for Etihad Rail facilities in Fujairah.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of huge water tanks as part of Etihad Rail's 2D package in Massaffi, Fujairah. The tanks provide essential water storage for rail operations and facilities.

## Key Features

- Large-capacity water tank construction
- Water storage infrastructure for rail facilities
- Pumping and distribution systems
- Water treatment integration
- Fire protection water supply
