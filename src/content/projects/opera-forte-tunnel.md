# Opera Tunnel & Forte Tunnel Downtown Dubai

Twin tunnel construction project in Downtown Dubai, providing critical underground access and reducing surface traffic congestion.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of two road tunnels (Opera and Forte) in Downtown Dubai, improving traffic flow around the Dubai Opera District and supporting the area's development.

## Key Features

- Two separate tunnel structures
- Underground ventilation systems
- Emergency evacuation passages
- Advanced lighting and safety systems
- Traffic control and monitoring systems

## Technical Specifications

- Opera Tunnel: 450 meters length
- Forte Tunnel: 380 meters length
- Cross-section: 2 lanes + emergency lane
- Ventilation: Jet fans with CO monitoring
- Safety: Emergency exits every 100 meters
