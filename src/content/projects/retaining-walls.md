# Retaining Walls

Reinforced concrete retaining wall construction project for grade separation and slope protection in Abu Dhabi.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of reinforced concrete retaining walls to provide grade separation and slope protection along roadways and developed areas.

## Key Features

- Reinforced concrete retaining walls
- Drainage systems behind walls
- Decorative finishes on exposed surfaces
- Safety barriers and fencing
- Landscaped areas above walls

## Technical Specifications

- Wall height: 3 to 6 meters
- Total length: 400 meters
- Foundation: Spread footings on prepared subgrade
- Drainage: Weep holes and drainage layers
- Finish: Exposed aggregate or painted concrete
