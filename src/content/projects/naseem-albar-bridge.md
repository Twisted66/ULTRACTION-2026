# Construction of Naseem Albar Bridge

Major bridge construction project connecting key areas in Abu Dhabi with modern infrastructure and enhanced traffic flow.

## Project Overview

The Naseem Albar Bridge is a significant infrastructure project designed to improve connectivity and reduce traffic congestion in Abu Dhabi. The bridge features modern design elements and accommodates multiple lanes of traffic.

## Key Features

- Multi-span bridge structure with precast concrete girders
- 6-lane roadway capacity
- Pedestrian walkways with safety barriers
- Advanced LED street lighting system
- Decorative architectural elements

## Technical Specifications

- Total length: 450 meters
- Width: 35 meters (6 lanes + shoulders)
- Foundation: Deep pile foundation system
- Superstructure: Precast concrete girders with cast-in-situ deck

## Construction Highlights

- Precast concrete segments for faster construction
- Night-time concrete pours to minimize traffic disruption
- Advanced geometric control for alignment accuracy
