# Huge Water Reservoir Riyadh City

Large-scale water storage facility built to serve the Riyadh City district of Abu Dhabi with reliable water supply.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of a massive underground water reservoir to enhance water storage capacity for the Riyadh City area, ensuring consistent supply during peak demand periods.

## Key Features

- Underground reinforced concrete reservoir
- Advanced waterproofing systems
- Pumping station with multiple pumps
- Water quality monitoring systems
- Emergency overflow and drainage

## Technical Specifications

- Capacity: 30 million gallons
- Dimensions: 80m x 60m x 10m deep
- Construction: Reinforced concrete with specialized waterproofing
- Pump capacity: 3,500 m3/hour
- Coating: Epoxy-based internal waterproofing
