# Water Reservoir & Retaining Walls Yas Island

Infrastructure project on Yas Island combining water storage facilities with extensive retaining wall systems.

## Project Overview

This project involved constructing a water reservoir and extensive retaining wall systems on Yas Island, providing critical infrastructure for the development.

## Key Features

- Underground water reservoir
- Reinforced concrete retaining walls
- Storm water drainage systems
- Access roads and parking
- Landscaped areas

## Technical Specifications

- Reservoir capacity: 15 million gallons
- Wall heights: 4 to 8 meters
- Total wall length: 800 meters
- Drainage: Comprehensive storm water system
- Access: 2km of internal roads
