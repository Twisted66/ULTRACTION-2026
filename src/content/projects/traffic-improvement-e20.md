# Traffic Improvement on E20 and Construction of 2 Bridges

Major road infrastructure project improving traffic flow on E20 highway with the construction of two new bridges and associated improvements.

## Project Overview

This project involved significant improvements to the E20 highway in Dubai, including the construction of two new bridges to replace at-grade intersections, improve traffic flow, and enhance safety.

## Key Features

- Two new bridge structures
- Highway widening and realignment
- New traffic interchanges
- Improved drainage systems
- Enhanced signage and lighting

## Technical Specifications

- Bridge 1: 180m span, 6 lanes
- Bridge 2: 140m span, 4 lanes
- Highway widening: 4km total length
- New lanes: 8 additional lanes
- Improvements: Drainage, lighting, signage, and barriers
