# Pump Stations & Retaining Walls

Infrastructure project combining water pump stations with reinforced concrete retaining walls for grade separation and water management.

## Project Overview

This project involved the construction of water pump stations alongside reinforced concrete retaining walls, creating an integrated infrastructure solution for water management and grade separation.

## Key Features

- Water pump stations with multiple pumps
- Reinforced concrete retaining walls
- Underground pipework and valves
- Control rooms and switchgear rooms
- Access roads and parking areas

## Technical Specifications

- Pump capacity: 4 x 300 m3/hour
- Retaining wall height: 6 meters maximum
- Wall length: 500 meters total
- Control systems: SCADA integration
- Construction: Reinforced concrete with waterproofing
