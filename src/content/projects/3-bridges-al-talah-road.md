# Development of 3 Bridges Al Talah Road

Triple bridge construction project along Al Talah Road, significantly improving traffic flow and connectivity in Dubai.

## Project Overview

This project involved the design and construction of three separate bridges along Al Talah Road, each engineered to handle specific traffic requirements and enhance overall road capacity.

## Key Features

- Three independent bridge structures
- Combined 12-lane capacity across all bridges
- Dedicated pedestrian walkways
- Modern architectural design with LED lighting
- Advanced traffic monitoring systems

## Technical Specifications

- Bridge 1: 180m span, 4 lanes
- Bridge 2: 220m span, 4 lanes
- Bridge 3: 160m span, 4 lanes
- Foundation: Drilled shafts with pile caps
- Superstructure: Prestressed concrete girders
