# Marine Staging Yassat Island

Specialized marine staging facility constructed at Yassat Island to support offshore construction and logistics operations.

## Project Overview

This marine staging facility was built to provide a base for offshore operations, including material storage, equipment staging, and vessel support for projects in the Abu Dhabi waters.

## Key Features

- Marine berth for construction vessels
- Storage areas for construction materials
- Equipment staging and maintenance areas
- Office and welfare facilities
- Fuel and water supply systems

## Technical Specifications

- Berth length: 250 meters
- Storage area: 15,000 square meters
- Office building: 2-story facility
- Utilities: Water, electricity, and communications
- Crane capacity: 100-ton mobile crane capability
